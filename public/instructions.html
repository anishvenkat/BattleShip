BATTLESHIP INSTRUCTIONS

  • Enter your name and press 'Start' to play.
  • If you want to start/join a private game, enter the gameID you want.
  • Tell the optional gameID to the friend, you want to play with.
  • Do not disclose your entered name to the opponent.
  • On the next page you have to place the ships whereever you want.
  • You get 5 ships to place.
  • 'Ready' button will activate once you place all your ships.
  • You can say ready or again place your ships by pressing on 'RESET' button.
  • For that you have to select a ship,allignment and starting point by clicking on the grid.
  • After placing the ship game will start.
  • Alternately both player will get a chance to guess enemy's ship location and hit.
  • Who ever will kill all the enemy's ship first will be the winner..
